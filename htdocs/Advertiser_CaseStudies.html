When digital content is all around us, why, then, should an ad network be restrictive to let you communicate only on a particular device?

This led us to build ‘Ripple’, an ad network for all smart screens so that marketers spend their time in creating engaging device agnostic experiences and stories and let our platform solve the challenges of integration and building screen specific extensions.
