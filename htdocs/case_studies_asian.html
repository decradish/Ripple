Asian Paints: Can intrigue drive engagements?

Objective - Build a stronger connotation of colour with Asian Paints

Asian Paints executed a whole new branding strategy with a radical new logo design as well a change in their communication to "Experience what colour can do for you". The objective was to use the personal nature of the mobile device to create a synonimity between colour and using Asian Paints.

Using a communication that hooked the users' attention within a contracted banner the moment they came across it on their mobile screens, the users were allured into clicking the banner using alluring, mysterious messages that generated curiosity.

On clicking the contracted banner, the users experienced a full screen ad in black and white that they could wipe off with their finger to fill their screen with colour and the Asian Paints branding and communication, hence portraying how the brand could fill their lives with colour.

Results - High engagement, exciting first impressions!

The ad created an engaging, lasting experience for the users with an engagement rate of over 6%
