Man of Steel C How to deliver Super creatives

Objective C Create a buzz before the big premiere

With the much awaited launch of Christopher Nolan and Zack Snyders Man of Steel, movie goers and comic lovers alike were eager in anticipation. The objective was to amplify the buzz around the movie and generate excitement around the premiere.

Idea C Better creatives make exciting ads

The Thunder format by Ripple allowed the movie to present an indulgently large amount of information to the users within a single ad unit.

Users could read a synopsis for the movie, view a gallery of stills from it, access the social media profiles for the film and click-through to the website for the same.

This way, a wholesome pre-movie experience was created for the users in the form of overlay microsites on a pre-roll video ad.

Results C Creating Super engagements

The campaign received an astounding response amongst the users, prompting them to interact with the creatively indulgent ad, resulting in an extremely impressive engagement rate of over 44%
