Rich Media Suite

Samsung - Experience the Note II on your OWN phone!

https://vimeo.com/69593852

Objective - Effectively display the features of the New Galaxy Note

Samsung Note II arrived with a bang, and how. The exciting new phone was packed to the brim with never-seen-before features and the brand wanted to ensure these features were effectively communicated to the right audiences, regardless of what devices they were using.

Idea - Bring the note experience on to the user's phone screens

Within the Rich Media creative, we employed Ripple's Rich Media Suite capability to recreate the look and feel of a Samsung Galaxy Note II within the screen of the smartphone the uses were using to view the rich media ad.

The animations in the ad recreated the actual functionality of the Note II, hence giving users a little taste of what their smartphone experience could be like, jumping to the Note II from the phone they were using to view the ad.

Results - A one-of-its-kind user experience

The ad resulted in a large number of mobile users experiencing the most exciting features of the new Galaxy Note II right on their current handsets.
