ROBUST & COMPLETE SDK

> The Ripple SDK is the most robust and comprehensive SDK to meet all your needs. We understand that while you would usually integrate ad network SDK’s to maximise earnings, our SDK allows you to do that with ease while also providing for app usage analytics and downloads attribution analytics. Our SDK has three modules

  • Advertising – This helps you serve the multiple banner, video, rich media & notification style ad units from Ripple

  • App Analytics – This helps you understand your app users better through analysis of usage, time spent, content and much more

  • Downloads – This helps to get a good understanding of app downloads and the efficacy of all promotion inventory sources you might use
