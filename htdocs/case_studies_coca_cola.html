Coca-Cola: Bringing the family together

https://vimeo.com/69604278

Objective - Provide interesting, relevant branded content to users around the campaign theme

Coca-cola initiated a campaign revolving around the concept of the product being the centre of family gatherings at the dinner table, the only time the entire family gets together to enjoy some quality time together.

The objective was provide an informative experience for the user around the aforementioned campaign.

Idea - A wholesome ad experience with maximum information

The rich media experience allowed users access to some of the most exciting, easy-to-prepare recipes from the kitty of coke's campaign website, Coke and Meals. In addition to this, they could download the recipes, as well as view the video ad campaign around the concept, and all of this information with a single rich media ad unit.

Results - Spectacular campaign, delicious results

The campaign garnered over 55,000 engagements over a short period and created a perfect disseminator of Coke and Meals' communication.
