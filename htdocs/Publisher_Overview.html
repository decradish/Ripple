Overview

RETAIN CONTROL

> Most publishers would look at ads as a necessary evil. You strive to get high ad CTRs to make more money but high CTRs also mean that users leave your site/app to reach the advertisers page

> We believe that as the traffic owner you should have greater control on your traffic without compromising on your ad earnings. We have thus built all our ad formats which are either In-banner or In-stream. This ensures that most engagements on ads can happen within the ad unit itself and your users continue to enjoy your site/app for longer

ENHANCE EXPERIENCE

> We believe that engaging ads can add value to your site/app experience and make it more fun. We do it by building impactful ad unitsbuilt over our robust RMS platform which makes ads more fun

> Our Publisher Development team works like your consultant and would share best practices and insights to identify the most impactful ad placements and ad units to enhance experience and maximise revenue

HIGH eCPMs

INTEGRATED SOLUTIONING
