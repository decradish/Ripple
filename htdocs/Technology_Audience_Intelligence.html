Audience Intelligence

We believe that reaching out to audiences without the proper amount of intelligence is like shooting an arrow in the dark. We believe that with the right kind of audience information, our advertisers can create campaigns that are smarter and more effective, thus maximising advertising value even for our users.

The Ripple platform utilizes its own data collected from users as well as third-party user data to create a comprehensive user information database for our advertisers.

The Ripple platform provides the users with:

> A large aggregation of audience information collected in-house and through third-party sources

> A wide network of affinity channels to effectively target audience interest groups

> Retargeting capability to create a holistic campaign experience for each individual user and, in the potential future, the ability to retarget user across devices used by them, as we believe no user in the age of diverse technologies should be reached out to on a single device
