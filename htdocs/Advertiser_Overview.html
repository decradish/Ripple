BUILD BRANDS

> Ripple’s Rich Media suite allows you to build engaging experiences which help maximise impact for your communication

> Innovative Ad Units like Rich Media Interstitials & Page Takeovers would help you get noticed and stand out

> Ripple Video advertising solutions are geared towards making your video ads more impactful and engaging, across screens

> Our Brand solutions team works like your consultant and would share best practices and insights to maximise impact for your brand campaign. Connect with us at advertise@ripple.ad for customised solutions

> On premium publisher partners on the Ripple network we offer Placement, Position and Sponsorship opportunities to maximise impact for your campaigns

> The Ripple platform ensures that campaigns are delivered only on brand safe content ensuring a better environment for your campaigns

DRIVE ENGAGEMENTS

> Ripple’s Rich Media suite allows you to build custom experiences which maximise impact for your communication

> Innovative ad units like In-banner games & In-video Key Moment based interactivities would help you build engagements with both In-banner & In-stream ads

> All rich media engagements on Ripple are delivered In-banner or In-video thus ensuring that we deliver enhanced experiences around content which consumers already like rather than taking them to newer destinations

> Our Brand solutions team works like your consultant and would share best practices and insights to maximise engagements for your brand. Connect with us at advertise@ripple.ad forthe most relevant solutions

REACH RELEVANT AUDIENCE

> As online advertising continues its rapid growth, audience buying is becoming more sophisticated. We understand that you should connect with your ideal potential customer displaying the right ad to convince them and thusachieve your marketing objectives.We help do that through our extensive targeting tools

> We have divided our audience basis affinity groups so that you choose the content types on which your ads run. You could buy the Sports affinity channel or Technology or several others which are sales team could advise you on

> Our Real Time Bidding (RTB) platform allows you to optimise bids for each impression bought over multiple publishers, applications and other supply sources with each impression being bid for optimally for the audience most relevant for you. This ensures that your campaign reaches to the audience which matters most in the most cost efficient way

MAXIMISE COST EFFICIENCY

> The Ripple platform allows you to buy inventory at fixed prices per impression or click or take advantage of our RTB platform to ensure that every impression bought is at the most optimal price point across billions of available impressions.

> Our platform is already integrated with top applications, publishers, and other inventory supply sources etc. to deliver the most cost efficient impression delivery vs. buying at standard prices. This is done through our smart algorithms for optimising programmatic buying and several self learning tools which make the platform richer and more capable to deliver more optimised campaigns

DRIVE DOWNLOADS

> Easy to integrate download tracker helps track downloads by source

> Insightful & Actionable analytics to understand download trends with specific attribution information

> Access to global inventory on top publisher sites and on over 30,000 popular applications

> Availability of billions of targeted monthly impressions across geographies to deliver extensive scale

> Rich Media Suite to make your app ads stand out and get users to experience key features of the app prior to the download itself. This ensures not only a higher download count but continued higher usage as the app has been downloaded with greater pre research

> Intelligent real time bidding (RTB) platform to maximise impression to click to download ratio

INTEGRATED SOLUTIONING
